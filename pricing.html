Frequently asked questions

Can I really use Solo for free forever?

Yes. Solo is genuinely useful, not a frustrating demo. It's perfect for students, side projects, or your first client. No credit card required, ever.

What happens if I hit my project limit?

Solo allows unlimited clients but 1 active project; Freelancer raises that to 5, and Studio is unlimited. When you're ready for the next project, upgrading takes seconds and your data carries over.

Is there a free trial on the paid plans?

Yes. Freelancer and Studio both come with a 14-day free trial. A card is required to start the trial, and you can cancel anytime before it ends to avoid being charged.

How do my clients pay me?

Every plan includes a public invoice page where clients can pay by card (Stripe) or PayPal, or you can simply share cash or bank-transfer instructions. Payments flow directly to you.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts and no cancellation fees: downgrade to Solo or cancel completely from your billing settings whenever you like.

What's coming next?

We're actively building GitHub/GitLab integration, a scope-creep / change-request module, a secure credential vault, income forecasting, and the Agency tier for teams. Items marked "soon" on the plans above are on the way.

Ready to weave it all together?

Start free on Solo, or take a paid plan for a 14-day spin. No lock-in, cancel anytime.
