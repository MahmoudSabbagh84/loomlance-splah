Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really use Solo tier forever for free? ▼
Absolutely! We believe every developer should have access to professional tools. The Solo tier is genuinely useful, not a frustrating demo. Perfect for students, side projects, or your first client. No credit card required, ever.
What happens if I exceed my project limit? ▼
On Solo, you can have unlimited clients but only 1 active project. When you're ready for project #2, you'll need to upgrade to Freelancer. We'll give you plenty of warning and make the transition smooth.
How does the GitHub/GitLab integration work? ▼
Link your repositories to projects and automatically track commits, pull requests, and deployment status. Show clients real progress with visual timelines. Perfect for demonstrating value and justifying your rates.
What's the "Scope Creep" module? ▼
Every developer's nightmare solved! When clients request changes outside the original scope, create professional change requests with time/cost estimates. Get approval before you code, then bill automatically. No more free work!
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes! No contracts, no cancellation fees. You can downgrade to Solo anytime or cancel completely. We're confident you'll love LoomLance, but we won't trap you if it's not the right fit.
When will the Studio tier be available? ▼
We're actively developing the Studio tier with advanced team features, analytics, and white-labeling capabilities. We expect to launch it in Q2 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when it's ready!
Do you offer discounts for students or non-profits? ▼
We're working on special programs! For now, the Solo tier is perfect for students. For established non-profits doing amazing work, reach out to us - we love supporting good causes.
