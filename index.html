About Us

At LoomLance, we believe freelancers shouldn't have to waste precious hours stitching together scattered tools just to run their business. That's why we created a single, seamless dashboard where you can manage clients, contracts, tasks, and invoices — all in one place.

Freelancers are the backbone of today's creative and digital economy. Designers, developers, writers, consultants, and creators all share the same struggle: too many tabs, too many apps, and not enough time to focus on what matters most — the work itself.

LoomLance was built to change that. Our mission is to weave the freelance experience into one smooth flow, helping you stay organized, get paid faster, and keep your client relationships strong.

We're not just another management tool — we're your digital workspace, your accountability partner, and your biggest supporter as you grow your independent career. With LoomLance, you can spend less time chasing invoices and juggling deadlines, and more time doing the work you love.
